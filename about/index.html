Athlete & Artist

I am a stylistically versatile performer and choreographer who embodies entertainment and storytelling. Hip-hop naturally flows through my soul with a passionate love and loyally continues to inspire my grounded modern-like movement. With experience dancing to various genres of music, I fully welcome all styles of music into my creativity.


My former experience participating in a competitive dance studio ignited my affinity for the concept of dancing thematically. My love for elements of intrigue, drama, and action drives me to pull in audiences with storytelling and entertainment. Choreographic motifs allow me to attach profound meaning and add a third dimension of significance to my composition. The use of unified movement between dancers combined with choreographic layers enables me to visually portray dynamic and immersive entertaining stories.


Firsthand experience as a lighting designer gives me a unique perspective as I compose new works. Incorporating audio, costuming, and lighting is a very rewarding and important process to what I produce, and I pay close attention to details. Production elements allow me to achieve my goal of immersion and bring the audience to a state of feeling as if they are a part of the action.
